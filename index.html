Who are we...

We are a cat cafe located in Beijing, China. Our main goal is to help stray cats find their own homes. Meow Cafe offers a variety of delicious coffees and desserts. This is definitely a perfect place for you and your friends to relax. Our cats are friendly, so don't worry about them attacking you. Please take them home if you can!

If you are looking for a special coffee shop, come to our cats! It's also worth to mention that our shop assistant are interesting people. They can also help you organize activities with your cats! Tip: 20% off if you are an MSU alumni!
